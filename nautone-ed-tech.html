What is education technology?

Educational technology also referred to as Edtech is the combined use of computer hardware, software, and traditional educational practices to facilitate learning. The companies that create these technologies are known as Edtech companies.

There are different companies in the edtech space and a majority of them are startups. Everybody takes a different approach to education and you can buy their products through their website.
