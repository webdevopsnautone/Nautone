Marketing campaigns done by companies to sell properties such as plots or apartments are called real estate marketing.

What are some of the real estate marketing strategies?

There are several real estate marketing strategies. Cold calling, banners, posters, visual media advertising, and cold emails are some of the traditionally followed strategies.
