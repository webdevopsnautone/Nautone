About Us

Who are we?

Businesses enter the market to solve a problem. Nautone takes a major chunk of work off their plate by taking care of their sales, marketing and support needs while they focus on making their product better.

What is it we want to achieve?

Nautone aims to be the one stop shop for any business by serving them from A to Z in terms of marketing, sales and support.

Where do we want to be?

We want to serve the sales and marketing needs of at least 10,000 businesses by the end of 2022.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is education technology?

Educational technology also referred to as Edtech is the combined use of computer hardware, software, and traditional educational practices to facilitate learning. The companies that create these technologies are known as Edtech companies.

Where to buy edtech products?

There are different companies in the edtech space and a majority of them are startups. Everybody takes a different approach to education and you can buy their products through their website.

How to sell online courses?

In the competitive edutech space, marketing and selling online courses is hard. Nautone can sell these courses with our sales and marketing experts on behalf of you.

What are some of the leading ed-tech companies in India?

Byju’s, Udemy, UpGrad, Vedantu, (and some more of our clients)

What does Nautone do for edtech companies?

Nautone analyzes your edtech product, understands it and sells it on behalf of you. We take care of identifying the audience and the sales and marketing part for your edtech courses.
